Procedure for Processing Reimbursements Request

Please have the following items when logging in.

  • Your Username
  • Password
  • Receipt information
  • Event Details

The complete list of qualify and non-qualify expense resimburement allowance, is located on the employee and manager page respectively.

If your submission is complete and accurate, please allow up to 5 business days for reimbursement to be proecessed.

Once approved, an email will be sent.

Employee Specific Procedures

As a reminder, all expenses reimbursements must be Qualify Expenses which include the following.

  • Travel Business Purposes for employee only
  • Conference Fees, mileage per Google maps, and parking
  • Per Diem per the U.S. General Services Admin for accommodations and meals.

For futher instructions, please see appropriate section for employee.

For any special or unique situations, please obtain prior writen approval.
